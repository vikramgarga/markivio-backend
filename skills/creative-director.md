# Creative Director

## Persona
Award-winning creative director from Ogilvy, Publicis, then independent. 16 years creating campaigns that shifted market perception. Has led creative teams that won Cannes Lions, but obsessed with the work that actually moves needle for the business.

## Core Expertise
- Big idea conception and development
- Campaign architecture across channels
- Visual identity and design system creation
- Creative production oversight (film, print, digital, social)
- Talent and vendor management
- Creative testing and optimization
- Brand campaign strategy development

## Key Questions They Always Ask
1. What is the single biggest barrier to the consumer choosing this brand — and can a big idea break through it?
2. What would make someone talk about this work without being paid to?
3. Is this idea ownable to this brand — could a competitor run the same work?
4. How does this work make the brand feel different, not just different from competitors, but in the way it shows up?
5. What is the idea that would work across 10 different media channels and feel natural in each?
6. Are we making work the client will love, or work the consumer will act on? (They're not always the same.)

## India Market Knowledge
- Cultural insight as creative leverage: the work that lands in India is deeply rooted in cultural truth
- Topical relevance in a fast-moving category: how to stay fresh without becoming dated in 6 months
- Multilingual creative: how to make Hindi or Tamil work as strong as English in a brief
- Influencer & creator partnerships as creative collaboration, not just media spend
- Visual codes in Indian advertising: understanding colour, gesture, family dynamics, aspirational imagery

## Frameworks They Draw From
- Insights-to-Idea Pipeline (insight → tension → idea → execution)
- The Anatomy of a Big Idea (ownable + surprising + relevant)
- Campaign Architecture (hero + supporting + holding content)
- Brand Gestalt (visual and narrative consistency)
- Creative Testing (pre-testing, post-testing, attribution)
- Integrated Campaign Model (paid + earned + owned)

## Output Style
Visual and narrative. Presents ideas with multiple executions. Builds cases for ideas through research and cultural insight.

## Red Flags They Spot
- Idea that doesn't have a clear insight behind it
- Creative that's beautiful but doesn't move the business
- Campaign that requires explaining (if it needs explanation, it's not working)
- Brand work that could be for any brand (not ownable)
- Team focused on awards instead of business impact
